About Us

who we are

We protect your home, business, and life with expert insurance advice and assistance.

Surge Insurance Brokers Limited (SIB) is an indigenous insurance broking firm incorporated as a limited liability company in the year 2022 and duly licensed by the National Insurance Commission of Ghana (NIC) to provide a diverse range of services on all insurance lines to both the commercial and private sectors as well as providing personal life insurances in the same year

At SIB we provide effective client management program through effective time-bound processes, excellent professional and quality services. To provide an unparalleled insurance and risk experience, we tailor make each insurance cover to suit the basic needs of our clients. Our partnering insurance companies understand the need for diversity and through professionalism and experience we create tailor-made products for our clients.
